About Our School

A Bright Beginning for Every Child

Mehr Sabir Pre Primary School provides expert early childhood education under the Omar Bin Khattab Educational Trust, backed by our in-house ECCED Teacher Training Institute in Kausa, Mumbra.

🛡️

Safe & Secure Campus

CCTV cameras, security guards, and a clean, hygienic environment keep your child safe and comfortable every day.

🖥️

Smart Board Classrooms

Air-conditioned classrooms equipped with smart board technology for an engaging, modern learning experience.

👩‍🏫

Individual Attention

Professionally trained, ECCED-certified staff ensure every child receives the personalised care and guidance they deserve.

What We Offer

Programs & Highlights

From toddler play groups to kindergarten readiness — every program is designed to give your child the strongest possible start.

🎈

Play Group

For children aged 2–3 years. A joyful introduction to school through play, music, and creative activities that spark curiosity and confidence.

🌸

Nursery

Building early language, motor skills, and social habits for 3–4 year olds in a warm, structured, and child-friendly environment.

🎒

Jr. Kindergarten

Foundational literacy and numeracy for 4–5 year olds through smart board lessons, hands-on activities, and guided play.

🎓

Sr. Kindergarten

Preparing 5–6 year olds for primary school with advanced concepts, reading readiness, and independent thinking skills.

🏠

Day Care Centre

Safe, supervised, and engaging after-hours care for working parents. Trained caregivers in a hygienic, child-friendly space.

📜

ECCED Teacher Training

1-Year Diploma in Pre-Primary Teacher Training. Two batches per year (January & July). Open to all 18+ with 10+2 qualification.

Our Strengths

Why Choose Mehr Sabir?

Qualified Teachers

Our educators hold D.T.Ed certifications and bring years of hands-on experience in early childhood development.

Safe & Nurturing Space

A secure, child-friendly campus where every student feels welcomed, comfortable, and encouraged to grow.

Activity-Based Learning

Learning through play, art, and hands-on activities that spark curiosity and build a strong foundation for life.

Personal Attention

Small class sizes ensure every child receives individual care, support, and focus from their teacher every day.

Affordable Fees

Quality early education made accessible to every family in the community, with transparent and reasonable fees.

Teacher Training (D.T.Ed)

We also offer a 1-year Diploma in Pre-Primary Teacher Training — two batches per year, open to all 18+ applicants.
